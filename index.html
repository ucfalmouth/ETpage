Educational Technology at Falmouth

  • About us

    We are a small team dedicated to guiding you in your use of technology for learning and teaching. We look after the Learning Space and provide consultation concerning best practice within online learning. We are keen to collect as much feedback from staff and students as possible. Your feedback is essential to improving the Learning Space.

  • Consultations

    We recommend a consultation once a term. This is a chance for your team to discuss ways of enhancing both your teaching experience and the students' learning experience. Prior to consultation, a member of ET will have explored your online material and may suggest course design improvements, user experience tips, or alternative tools.

    The consultations have proven beneficial as both an exchange of ideas and as an opportunity for ET to learn a great deal about the differing requirements of Falmouth University's departments. We provided thirteen consultations last term and are confident that the process has been beneficial to those managing the courses, as well as Educational Technology.

    Book a consultation

  • Individual & Group Training

    The training offered avoids the 'one-size-fits-all' approach so prevalent in technical disciplines. The level of proficiency and number of participants is highly flexible, allowing individuals or entire teams to be catered for. Content is aimed at creating courses on the Learning Space, through to broad advice concerning emerging technologies and platforms.

    Speak to the team

  • Drop ins

    Educational Technology have been providing drop-in sessions over the last year, aimed at both staff and students. Situated in prominent locations across campus, members of Team ET were available to answer questions, offer advice and collect essential feedback from all those currently using the Learning Space.
